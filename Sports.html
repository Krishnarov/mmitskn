FACILITIES & RESOURCES

SPORTS & PHYSICAL EDUCATION

At Mahamaya Polytechnic of Information Technology, we believe that physical education is an integral part of holistic development. Our institute provides excellent sports facilities to encourage students to participate in various indoor and outdoor games. Regular physical activities not only keep students physically fit but also help them develop team spirit, leadership qualities, and stress management skills.

The sports department organizes various inter-class and inter-collegiate tournaments throughout the academic year. Our students actively participate in district, state, and national level competitions and have brought laurels to the institute on numerous occasions.

OUTDOOR SPORTS FACILITIES

Football Ground

Well-maintained football field with proper markings and goalposts. The ground hosts regular practice sessions and inter-collegiate tournaments.

Cricket Field

Standard cricket pitch with practice nets where students can hone their batting, bowling, and fielding skills under the guidance of experienced coaches.

Volleyball & Basketball Courts

Dedicated courts with proper infrastructure for volleyball and basketball games. These courts are actively used for practice and competitions.

Badminton & Tennis Courts

Well-maintained courts with proper lighting for badminton and lawn tennis, allowing students to play even after regular college hours.

Athletics Track

400-meter track for various athletic events including sprints, middle and long-distance running, hurdles, and relay races.

Fitness Area

Open-air fitness zone with basic equipment for physical exercises, warm-up, and cool-down routines before and after sports activities.

INDOOR SPORTS FACILITIES

Table Tennis

Multiple tables available for practice and competitions. The institute regularly organizes table tennis tournaments for students and staff.

Chess & Carrom

Dedicated area for chess and carrom board games that help students develop strategic thinking and concentration skills.

Gymnasium

Well-equipped gym with modern fitness equipment for strength training, cardio exercises, and overall physical fitness.

ANNUAL SPORTS EVENTS

Annual Sports Meet

A week-long event held every February featuring various athletic and team events. Students compete in track and field events, team sports, and individual games to showcase their sporting talents.

Inter-Branch Tournaments

Regular competitions between different branches of the institute to foster healthy competition and branch spirit. These tournaments include cricket, football, volleyball, and basketball.

Technical Education Department Games

Our students represent the institute in various sports competitions organized by the Technical Education Department at district and state levels.

Friendly Matches

Regular friendly matches with neighboring educational institutions to provide exposure and competitive experience to our students.
