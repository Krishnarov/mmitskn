ABOUT COLLEGE

WELCOME TO MMIT, SANT KABIR NAGAR

Mahamaya Polytechnic of Information Technology (MMIT) , Sant Kabir Nagar is one of the prestigious technical institute of learning in Sant Kabir Nagar district in Uttar Pradesh. This institution was established in the year 2016, and is situated at a distance of 10 Kms from the Khalilabad railway station on Mehdawal road. The institute aims to deliver technical education and imparting skill to the students of all the sections of the society. The prime focus of the institute is to empower students with sound knowledge, wisdom, and training both at the academic and competitive industrial market. This institute is affiliated to Board of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh. M.M.I.T. Sant Kabir Nagar offers a diploma in three branches: Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics Engineering & Electrical Engineering. All courses are three year diploma programme approved by AICTE, New Delhi. The institution has a well furnished library with 3000+ books comprising of both course related and reference books. There are four smart classes in the institute and well equipped labs for all the respective branches. The institution has facilities for both indoor and outdoor games.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver quality education to the students with an aim to achieve excellence in teaching and learning. The institute aims at providing skill oriented training to the students to meet the needs of the society and industry.

Our Vision

To attain excellence in technical education, facilitating academic growth and skill development in the fields of science and technology.
